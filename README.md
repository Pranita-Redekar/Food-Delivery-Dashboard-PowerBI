<h1 align="center">🍔 Food Delivery Sales Dashboard</h1>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
This project presents an interactive <b>Food Delivery Sales Dashboard</b> built using Power BI.
The dashboard provides insights into order trends, customer preferences,
delivery performance, and payment behavior.
</p>

<p>
The goal is to transform raw food delivery data into meaningful insights
for better business decisions and operational efficiency.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Project Objectives</h2>
<ul>
<li>Analyze overall order quantity and transactions.</li>
<li>Track monthly sales performance.</li>
<li>Identify most popular food items.</li>
<li>Understand customer ordering patterns.</li>
<li>Evaluate delivery performance.</li>
<li>Analyze payment method usage.</li>
</ul>

<h2>📈 Dashboard Features</h2>

<h3>1. Quantity by Month</h3>
<p>
Bar chart showing monthly order quantity trends (January to April).
</p>

<h3>2. Transactions by Food Type & Member Type</h3>
<p>
Table comparing <b>Gold vs Regular customers</b> across food categories
like Main Course, Snacks, Desserts, Breakfast, and Beverages.
</p>

<h3>3. Top Selling Food Items</h3>
<p>
Horizontal bar chart highlighting popular items like:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Samosa</li>
<li>Butter Chicken</li>
<li>Chole Bhature</li>
<li>Masala Dosa</li>
<li>Gulab Jamun</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. Delivery Status Analysis</h3>
<p>
Donut chart showing percentage of <b>Delivered vs Cancelled</b> orders.
</p>

<h3>5. Payment Method Analysis</h3>
<p>
Bar chart showing usage of payment methods such as <b>UPI, COD, and Card</b>.
</p>

<h3>6. Transactions Trend</h3>
<p>
Line chart displaying transactions over months (Jan–Apr 2023).
</p>

<h2>📌 Key Insights</h2>
<ul>
<li>Samosa and Butter Chicken are top-selling food items.</li>
<li>Majority of orders are successfully delivered (~94%).</li>
<li>UPI is the most preferred payment method.</li>
<li>Main Course category generates highest transactions.</li>
<li>Gold members contribute significantly to total orders.</li>
</ul>
